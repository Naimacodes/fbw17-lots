GAMES

I like games even on a very basic level. Experimenting, figuring out, just playing with possibilities. I’ll probably go on about it later but there’s something that looks very important for development and this is being able to learn from trial and error. Real life isn’t so kind on that regard, you’re not going to learn to drive by smashing into trees. Games allow you to fail, get up, and try again, without having to make a trip to the hospital.
